Visit Abuja

Enjoy culture and cool vibes in this vibrant district in the Federal Capital Territory of Nigeria.

Top three activities to do in Abuja

Zuma Rock

This is what you can call “The Rock”. Zuma rock is a special and significant landmark. Zuma rock is a beautiful natural sight to behold.

Retro Africa Gallery

This is a favorite among the residents of Abuja. It is for every gallery lover who enjoys the beauty of art. Seasonally, they feature art exhibits from selected local and international artists.

Jabi Lake Mall

Do you love shopping? Well, this is a nice huge mall with views of Jabi Lake. This is a lovely place for foreigners to shop and enjoy good food and ambience.
